Janno Siim

I research non-interactive zero-knowledge proof systems and related topics (some time ago, also e-voting). I did my PhD at the University of Tartu under the supervison of Helger Lipmaa. Currently, I conduct postdoctoral research at the University of Edinburgh and (still) at the University of Tartu.
